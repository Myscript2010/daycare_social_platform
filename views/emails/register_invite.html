Over the past two years our team has been working hard to disrupt the traditional way parents find, communicate and cooperate with child care providers. As a result we created Kindzy.

Kindzy is a social platform that enables child care providers and parents stay connected. It is an on-line community where parents can find trusted child care providers and stay closely connected to them.

With Kindzy you will be able to:

  • create groups based on various criteria
  • have a database with the children enrolled, their parents and the staff that you have, with all the proper information attached to each of their profiles
  • keep parents updated with the daily activities children are involved in
  • contact individuals or groups via our private messaging system
  • conduct interactive discussions through posts
  • instantly post photos or share albums etc.

Your Kindzy profile page can serve as a reflection of your vision and presence in the community. It can also eliminate the need for a website and the related costs without compromising security and accessibility on the public Internet.
